## 🚀 Why Seed Mango?

Tired of fragile 3D-printed plastics or dropping $500+ on a single industrial servo? We were too. 
We built the Seed Mango to bridge the gap: delivering a true quasi-direct drive (QDD) experience with industrial-grade metal durability, priced for the open-source community.

## ⚙️ Core Specifications

Our 30:1 main configuration is physically tuned for the perfect balance of torque density and back-drivability. 

* **Mechanism:** Full CNC Metal Cycloidal Drive (30 teeth, 31 pins)
* **Nominal Torque:** 12 Nm (Perfect for sustained gravity compensation)
* **Peak Dynamic Torque:** 75+ Nm (Tested for high-impact movements)
* **Operating Voltage:** 24V - 36V optimized (Max 40V)
* **Encoders:** Dual Absolute Encoders
* **Ecosystem:** Fully ODrive-compatible (Supports FOC & MIT modes)

## 🛠️ Modularity & Ecosystem

The Seed Mango isn't just a motor; it's a universal mechanical platform. 
* **Custom Motor Stators:** Swap in different stators (e.g., 62BM26 / 80BM26) to shift from high-agility to heavy-payload configurations.
* **Active Cooling System:** Designed with side-venting paths to accommodate 2510 high-speed fans. We utilize the internal magnetic bracket as a centrifugal impeller to push continuous torque even higher while protecting the electronics.
* **Open Controllers:** We are partnering to adapt mature, fully-tested RP2350 FOC boards, ensuring rock-solid thermal reliability right out of the gate.
